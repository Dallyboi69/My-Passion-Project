Dungeons and Dragons for Beginners

With Dungeons & Dragons being at an all-time high in popularity in media like Baldur's Gate 3 and Stranger Things, more and more people are wanting to try playing it with their friends. This however can be a daunting task to the layman as there is so much to know. This website will act as a crash course to newbies to the Forgotten Realms.
