Dungeons and Dragons for Beginners

With Dungeons & Dragons being at an all-time high in popularity in media like Baldur's Gate 3 and Stranger Things, more and more people are wanting to try playing it with their friends. This however can be a daunting task to the layman as there is so much to know. This website will act as a crash course to newbies to the Forgotten Realms.

A Little About Me

Hi! I'm Dallin Blaine, an expert Dungeon Master and Dungeons & Dragons enthusiast with over 15 years of experience. Here are a few highlights of my qualifications and experience:

  • Dungeon Master Experience: 15+ years of DMing in multiple editions of D&D, including 3.5e, 5e, and 5.5e, with a focus on high-fantasy, gothic horror, and custom world-building.
  • Published Content Creator: Designed and published popular homebrew modules like "Whispers of the Fallen Kingdom" and several custom magical items.
  • Rule System Expertise: Proficient in multiple editions of D&D, including advanced knowledge of Pathfinder and Unearthed Arcana.
  • Storytelling & Player Engagement: Skilled at creating immersive narratives and balancing tactical gameplay to cater to all player styles.
  • Event Coordinator: Organized and led over 50 public D&D events, including tournaments and charity games at conventions like Gen Con and PAX.
  • Dungeon Master Trainer: Hosted workshops and tutorials for aspiring Dungeon Masters and players, helping over 200 participants master the game.
