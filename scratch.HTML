Dungeons and Dragons for Noobs

Welcome to my page about something I am passionate about! A lot of introductions to D and D start with rules and stats. I like to make it more simple...

  1. Who are you?
    • What kind of person would you be under different circumstances?
    • What do you wish you could be?
  2. Apply these questions as you select one of the DnD races and classes
